Viltrox AF 26mm f/2.8 EVO lens is a fast, compact full-frame pancake prime weighing around 130g (4.58oz) with a lens barrel length of only 23.8mm. Offering both focus and aperture rings, an STM autofocus motor, 0.2m close focus, and a 43mm front filter thread, the lens is now available for Sony E and Nikon Z cameras for $299. The Chinese lens manufacturer Viltrox has recently been announcing new lenses at an increasing pace. During the NAB Show in Las Vegas earlier this year, we talked with Viltrox about their upcoming products, and the company mentioned the EVO series of prime lenses.
