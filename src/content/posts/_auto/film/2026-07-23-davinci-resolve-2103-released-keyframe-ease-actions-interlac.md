Blackmagic Design has released DaVinci Resolve 21.0.3, a maintenance update that adds configurable keyframe ease actions and a legacy speed mode retime curve option while fixing issues across editing, Fusion, and scripting. The free update also addresses decoding of some Sony BURANO X-OCN clips and expands the scripting API. This is the third point release for version 21, the build Blackmagic Design constructed around the new Photo page and an expanded AI toolset, which we covered when Resolve 21 shipped. It follows version 21.0.1, focused on RAW decoding and HDR delivery, and version 21.0.2, which sharpened keyframing and retime behavior.
