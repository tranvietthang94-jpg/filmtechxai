Sony has released firmware version 5.00 for the VENICE 2, covering both the 8.6K (MPC-3628) and 6K (MPC-3626) sensor versions of its flagship CineAlta camera. The free update adds a new &#8220;Signature&#8221; Preset Look for monitoring, a 4096×2160P 12G SDI output format at a 47.95 fps project frame rate, and the ability to use the CNA-2 Camera Control Network Adaptor as a LAN conversion adaptor. The arrival of Sony VENICE 2 Firmware V5 is no surprise for anyone following Sony&#8217;s long-term firmware roadmap. The company confirmed earlier this year that VENICE 2 updates would continue through 2027, with V5.0 planned for 2026 and V6.0 to follow, as we reported in \[&#8230;\]
