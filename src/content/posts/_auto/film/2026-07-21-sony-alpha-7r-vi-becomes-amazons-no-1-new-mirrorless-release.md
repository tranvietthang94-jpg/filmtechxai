The Sony Alpha 7R VI has climbed to No. 1 on Amazon’s Hot New Releases list for Mirrorless Cameras. The body is currently shown at $4,498, and Amazon says more than 100 units were bought during the past month. That is an unusual position for a premium camera that costs nearly $4,500. It also arrives at almost the same price as Sony’s FX3A, turning a retail ranking into a larger camera market story. The ranking does not mean the Alpha 7R VI is Amazon’s overall best-selling mirrorless camera.
