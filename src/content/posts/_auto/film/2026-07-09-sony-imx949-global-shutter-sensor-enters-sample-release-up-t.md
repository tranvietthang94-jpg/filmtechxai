Sony Semiconductor Solutions has started communicating the sample release of its new IMX949 global shutter image sensor. According to Sony’s tentative product flyer, the IMX949-AQB is a Type 1.8, 12.69MP, Pregius S CMOS sensor with a 28.1mm diagonal, 5.48 μm pixels, and a maximum listed frame rate of 811.5 frames per second. That makes the IMX949 more than a 500 FPS sensor. Depending on bit depth, it becomes an 800 FPS-class global shutter sensor.
