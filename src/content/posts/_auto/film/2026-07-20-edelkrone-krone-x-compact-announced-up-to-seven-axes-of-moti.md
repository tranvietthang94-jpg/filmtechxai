edelkrone has unveiled the Krone X Compact, a robotic camera motion system that packs a dolly, a jib, and a motorized head into a setup small enough to travel inside a single backpack. It promises up to seven axes of synchronized, programmable movement for solo shooters and small crews, and it may be the company’s most portable multi-axis rig yet. Motion control has traditionally meant weight, cases, and setup time, which is exactly the barrier edelkrone has spent years chipping away at with its modular tools.
