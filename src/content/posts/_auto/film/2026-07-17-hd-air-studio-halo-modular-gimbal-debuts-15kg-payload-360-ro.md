HD Air Studio, the Lublin-based engineering house behind the Cyclo and Elit stabilized heads, has revealed HALO, a modular three-axis gimbal it describes as a handheld-first system with full remote-head capabilities. The design is rated to carry up to 15 kg / 33 lb, adds unlimited 360° roll and pan through integrated power and SDI slip rings, and is being aimed at cinema packages built around the ARRI ALEXA 35, ALEXA Mini LF, and Sony VENICE 2. HALO enters a category that has seen surprisingly little turnover at the top end.
