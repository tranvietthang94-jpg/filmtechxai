Tamron has launched the 12-20mm f/2.8 (Model A084), the widest full-frame lens the company has ever made and the first to debut its new Toned Profile Next lens design. The fast ultra-wide zoom for Sony E-mount and Nikon Z mount weighs just 570g / 20.2oz, focuses down to 0.18m / 7.1in, and features Tamron’s VXD linear autofocus motor. Tamron has been busy filling out its mirrorless lineup over the past year, from the 16-30mm f/2.8 Di III VXD G2 that completed the company’s f/2.8 zoom trinity to the versatile 25-200mm f/2.8-5.6 ultra-zoom for Sony E-mount.
