Swedish company LIT Systems has released LIT OS 0.9 beta4, the latest public test firmware for its LIT DUO 1 all-in-one light and color meter. The update brings 2.5x higher spectrum measuring resolution, new CRI and SSI readings, a new ΔEV exposure difference submode, faster measurements across the board, and a reworked Bluetooth protocol ahead of the final LIT OS 0.9 release, which is planned for mid-August 2026 at the latest. The LIT DUO 1 has been on our radar for a while now.
