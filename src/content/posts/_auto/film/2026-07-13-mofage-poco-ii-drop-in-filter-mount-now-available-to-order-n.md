MOFAGE has officially launched the second iteration of its POCO drop-in filter adapter. The MOFAGE POCO II retains the modularity of the original POCO, but adds an interchangeable PL/EF front lens mount, increased clearance for lenses with longer rear protruding elements, and a lighter, more compact design. Introduced in 2022, the MOFAGE POCO was initially released as a budget-friendly PL mount adapter for mirrorless camera systems with an integrated, drop-in variable ND. Still, it later evolved into a full ecosystem with new filters and camera mounts added along the way.
