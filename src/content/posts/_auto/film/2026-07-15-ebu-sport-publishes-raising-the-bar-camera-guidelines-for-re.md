EBU Sport has published “Raising the Bar”, a set of practical camera and broadcast direction guidelines aimed at ending the sexualization of women athletes in live athletics coverage. Developed with input from Olympic athletes Holly Bradshaw, Ivana Španović, and Blanka Vlašić, the free publication breaks down positive and negative camera positions, shot sizes, and replay practices for high jump, pole vault, horizontal jumps, and running events. For directors, camera operators, and VT operators working in live sports, this is a rare document: a broadcaster-driven style guide that addresses framing and replay decisions shot by shot, using illustrated examples drawn entirely from real broadcast coverage.
